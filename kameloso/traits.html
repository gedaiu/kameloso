    Module kameloso.traits

    Various traits that are too kameloso-specific to be in lu.

    They generally deal with lengths of struct member names, used to format output and align columns for kameloso.printing.printObject.

    Aliases

    longestMemberName longestMemberNameImpl!(No.all,Things) Gets the name of the longest configurable member in one or more structs.
    longestMemberTypeName longestMemberTypeNameImpl!(No.all,Things) Gets the name of the longest type of a member in one or more structs.
    longestUnserialisableMemberName longestMemberNameImpl!(Yes.all,Things) Gets the name of the longest member in one or more structs, including lu.uda.Unserialisable ones.
